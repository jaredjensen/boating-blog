--- layout: post title: "Rendezvous" subtitle: "1988 Ocean Alexander 420 Sundeck" date: 2024-12-06 background: /img/posts/bg-rendezvous.jpg preview: /img/posts/rendezvous-for-sale-42.jpg tags: rendezvous boats --- Rendezvous is an Ocean Alexander 42' aft cabin yacht with sundeck and flybridge. We purchased her in December 2021, upgrading from [Denovo]({% link _posts/2021-12-15-denovo-for-sale.html %}), a Ranger Tug R29. She is loaded with great equipment and has reliably taken us all around the Puget Sound, San Juan Islands, and Canada. We've put a lot of money and elbow grease into keeping her serviced and clean throughout. A few of those projects have their own posts on this site. We're once again looking for something a little bigger, so Rendezvous is now for sale. ## Specifications
- Length: 42' LWL / 46' LOA - Beam: 13'-11" - Draft: 3.5' - Engines: Twin Volvo TAMD61A - Total Horsepower: 612 HP - Sleeps: 6
- Cruising Speed: 9 knots - Maximum Speed: 17 knots - Fresh Water Tank: 150 Gallons - Fuel Tank: 300 Gallons - Holding Tank: 45 Gallons - Staterooms: 2 - Heads: 2 (1 with shower)
